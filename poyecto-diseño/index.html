Why use mind maps?

A mind map is a tool that translates thoughts into visuals and has many advantages over traditional note-taking and writing techniques.

Information on the structure of mind maps

Mind maps are graphic representations of information that communicate the relationship between individual concepts and ideas. No matter how complex or broad a topic is, a mind map brings order out of chaos and helps you understand "the big picture."

Mind maps improve memory

Mind maps use various memory triggers, such as colors and images, which are much easier for your brain to memorize and remember than information in text. In addition, mind maps help you link new information to your existing knowledge.

Mind maps promote creativity

There are very few techniques that can activate your creativity like mind maps. This is because mind maps use images and keywords to create new associations in your brain, which you can also transcribe with incredible speed.

Mind maps facilitate collaboration

While traditional mind maps made on simple sheets of paper are great for brainstorming individually, online mind maps allow you to brainstorm with others and make plans together and in real time, no matter where participants are or what. device they use.
